Scribe for meetings has a variety of plans and on-demand purchase options for individuals, companies and organizations.

## Meeting Pass

A meeting pass allows you to conduct a single meeting using Scribe for Meetings. Meeting passes do not expire.

## Individual Subscription

Individual subscriptions allow a user to conduct an unlimited amount of meetings. Individual subscriptions are available in monthly and yearly billing terms.

## Organization Subscription

Organizations of any size can acquire Scribe for Meetings via a fixed price monthly or yearly subscription.
